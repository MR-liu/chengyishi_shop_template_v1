About the MW08

A Leap Forward in Sound and Design.

Crafted from ceramic and stainless steel, the MW08 wireless earbuds features Hybrid Active Noise-Cancellation, a streamlined form designed for comfort, and a new wind-reducing talk solution with 6 microphones. These innovative active noise-cancelling wireless earphones will transform any listening experience.

Customers reviews

Oliver H.

Ideal

The sound from these in ears in amazing for their size. They have a surprisingly wide sound stage and good separation. The bass from the 11mm drivers is strong with good weight, but never overpowers the other elements even on bass heavy tracks. They have great detail too, I was noticing things in my favourite songs I hadnt heard before and they go plenty loud enough.

Andy W.

Well made Earbuds

I think that the MW08 earbuds are built very well, and they have a premium look and feel to them as well. Also, the sound is very clear and crisp, and they have a good sound stage as well. However I do wish that they were just a little bit louder and that they had EQ adjustment on their app. With that being said, I have played drums and guitar for years, so I am relatively sure that I have lost some hearing. Overall I am glad that I purchased them and would recommend them to anyone looking for audiophile grade wireless earbuds.

Victor M.
